About me

I have had a long and successful career in software development. I am essentially an architect and systems builder. I believe in the importance of understanding technology from a top-down perspectve and making sure that good high-level design is in place. Any system that is lacking a good top-level design will have much difficulty compensating for that lack at the lower levels.


I also believe that technology systems should be well-aligned with the business processes they enable and support. The meaning of technology is found in the business value they provide. Further, systems should always provide a gateway to the future - anticipating, enabling and facilitating progress. Technology systems should never be impediments to progress.
